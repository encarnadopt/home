Porto . Bolhao . BURGER

Encarnado Burger arrives in Porto with a focus on the meat, the texture and a burger experience shaped by flavour.

Encarnado was born in 2018 inside a container in Ipanema, Rio de Janeiro, and is now also in Botafogo. The house proposal is simple: a real burger. Soft brioche bun, fresh beef, quality cheese, sauces and sides made in-house.

About us

Made to get your hands dirty.

Encarnado Burger comes from a simple desire: serving burgers with presence, soft bread, juicy meat, melted cheese and sauce at the right point.

In Bolhao, Encarnado Burger brings together a central location and a straightforward experience: order, sit down, eat well and come back.
